    About

    All Cotton Bags is an ecologically motivated start up Ecommerce venture with the vision to provide eco-friendly solutions to customer and society.We believe in protecting the nature.

    Eco-friendly

    Our products are Eco-friendly, Recycled and Reusable. Therefore, you can rest assured that our products are as environmentally friendly as possible.

    Save Nature

    In order to save nature and also to maintain eco-friendly environment, all cotton bags manufacture was initiated.

    Conserve Natural Resources

    Cottons bags use less water, energy and oil. And have less impact on air-pollution. It is a perfect solution to conserve natural resources, keep oceans clean and save wild animals.

    Reusable

    Cotton bags are more sustainable and nice than plastic bags. They are easy to wash and you can reuse them.

    Cloth Bags

    Cloth bags are woven from thread and since cloth is a plant product, cloth bags are ultimately biodegradable, although most consumers choose to reuse the bags.

    Strength

    Cloth bags are thicker than plastic bags, which makes them strong enough to be used again and again. Cloth bags are larger than plastic bags, so they are capable of holding larger items.
